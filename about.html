About Us

Professional quantity surveying and construction project management expertise

Company Profile

KJ & Associates Consultancy Ltd is a locally registered Quantity Surveying firm operating in Tanzania for 17+ years. Our directors bring more than 15 years of combined experience in quantity surveying and construction project management.

We specialize in providing comprehensive quantity surveying services to both public and private sector clients, with particular expertise in donor-funded projects from organizations such as the World Bank, African Development Bank (AfDB), UNDP, and UNHCR.

Our firm is registered with the Architects and Quantity Surveyors Registration Board (AQRB) and maintains all necessary legal certifications including TIN, VAT registration, business license, and professional indemnity insurance.
