KJ & Associates Consultancy Ltd is a registered Quantity Surveying and Construction Project Management firm in Tanzania, licensed by the Architects and Quantity Surveyors Registration Board (AQSRB).

1. Services Overview

KJ & Associates Consultancy Ltd provides professional quantity surveying and construction project management services, including but not limited to:

  • Pre-contract services (cost planning, feasibility studies, bills of quantities)
  • Post-contract services (valuations, cost control, final accounts)
  • Tendering and contract documentation
  • Project management and administration
  • Dispute resolution and claims support
  • Technical advisory services

2. Professional Standards

All services provided by KJ & Associates Consultancy Ltd are delivered in accordance with:
