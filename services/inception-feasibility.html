Inception & Feasibility

Budget estimates, cost plans, and cash flow forecasts during project inception

What We Do

We provide comprehensive cost planning and feasibility analysis from the earliest stages of your project, ensuring informed decision-making and budget control. Our team works closely with project owners, developers, and funding agencies to establish realistic budgets and cost plans that guide the entire project lifecycle.

Key Services

  • Initial budget estimates based on project scope and requirements
  • Detailed cost planning and advice throughout feasibility studies
  • Cash flow forecasts to support funding applications
  • Feasibility cost analysis and value engineering recommendations
  • Cost benchmarking against similar projects
  • Risk assessment and contingency planning

Deliverables

Our inception and feasibility services deliver:

  • Comprehensive budget estimates with detailed breakdowns
  • Detailed cost plans aligned with project phases
  • Cash flow projections for funding and planning purposes
  • Feasibility reports with cost implications
  • Value engineering recommendations

Who It Helps

This service is essential for:

  • Project owners and developers requiring early-stage cost certainty
  • Funding agencies (World Bank, AfDB, UNDP, etc.) needing detailed cost analysis
  • Government agencies planning public infrastructure projects
  • Private developers evaluating project viability
  • Financial institutions assessing project funding requirements
